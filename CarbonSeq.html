Carbon Sequestration in the Amazon

Explore the potential for carbon offsetting and forest planning in the Amazon rainforest using Google Earth Engine. This application calculates Aboveground Carbon Density (ACD) and Gross Primary Production (GPP), providing insights into high carbon density areas and suggesting optimal locations for future forest conservation efforts.

Utilizing the latest data and geographic information systems, this project supports local government initiatives to combat climate change by identifying regions ideal for carbon sequestration and conservation planning.
